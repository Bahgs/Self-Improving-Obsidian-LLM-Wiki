# Bad Pull-Forward Questions

Bad pull-forward questions sound useful but do not improve future work.

## Example

Bad questions:

- What is AI?
- How can we learn more about this?
- What are the benefits of software?
- Should we research this more?

Why these fail:

- They are generic.
- They do not use the source's specific mechanism.
- They do not connect to an existing wiki cluster, project, or schema decision.
- They are unlikely to change future ingest behavior.
